## Senior Backend Architect & Technical Leader

This GitHub account is used for professional engineering work at **Verofy®**, an FCA-regulated fintech platform operating in the UK payments ecosystem.

Repositories under this account are **private by design** and relate to production systems handling regulated workflows, including secure onboarding, payment processing, and internal decision engines.

## What I work on

* **Payments & Regulated Fintech Systems**  
    Design and development of backend systems for enterprise payment platforms, built with a strong focus on compliance, reliability, and long-term maintainability.

* **Backend Architecture & APIs**  
    Translating complex business and regulatory requirements into clean, scalable APIs and service-oriented architectures used in real production environments.

* **Security & Data Protection**  
    Implementing practical security patterns across authentication, authorization, and sensitive data flows in line with regulated fintech standards.
